About Me

Hello! My name is Melissa Delgado, I am 24 years old and live in Roselle, Illinois. Currently, I'm working at Apple as a Technical Specialist, helping customers solve different product issues that may arise.

I've collaborated on two projects under the same leadership during my time at Northeastern Illinois University. As well as taking lead in smaller groups during the development of the project.

I was introduced into the world of UI/UX when my professor saw my passion for wanting to be able to use my Computer Science degree to use technology to help others.

I am very passionate about helping others as much as I possibly can! My main career goal is to leave a positive imprint in the technology field and show others how it can be a valuable resource in their everyday lives.
